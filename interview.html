Where did you spend your childhood?

"I was born in China and lived there till when I was fifth grade where I
then moved to Boston. So I would say I spend half of my childhood back
in China and with the other half in the States. I am very glad that I was
able to experience the two different cultures and enables me now to integrate
the two cultures into what I love and pursue which is a degree in travel and tourism."

What is your most memorable childhood experience?

"My most memorable childhood experience was definitely around
the Chinese New Years because that's when all our family gather
around for a week of celebration.We would visit other relatives’
home and not only receive red envelopes but would have lunch or dinner
together.The overall vibe and atmosphere is just indescribable.
But as I moved to the States to pursue my education,I have not
experience the authentic Chinese New Years for almost 8 years now."
