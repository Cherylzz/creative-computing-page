Conditional Design

Rules 1

1. Each player selects a color (Blue/Red/Yellow) and draws a solid circle on anywhere of the paper one at a time.
2. Right after a player drew their circle, then he/she uses a straw to blow the circle in any directions he/she wants with unlimited attempts, everyone takes turn.
3. Stop when two players are tired (out of oxygen)

Rules 2

1. Each player selects a color (Blue/Red/Yellow) and a shape (Square/Circle/Triangle).
2. One person each has a turn to draw their shape and use a hair dryer to blow their shapes until the paint is dried.
3. Each shape has to connect with one another.
4. The game will stop after six rounds.
